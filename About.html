About Neve

Bonjour!



      People call me Neve /nēv/ but my childhood nickname was ‘NN’ which stands for Neve-Nineveeh. The name ‘Neve’ was from an artist and ‘Nineveeh’ was from the story of Jonah found in the Bible. I’m an 18 year old life living in Cavite, Philippines and is currently studying at De La Salle University – Dasmariñas taking up Bachelor of Science in Psychology. I love spending my free time being a couch potato watching k-dramas, series, movies and vlogs and I’m definitely not a sporty person. Aside from all those romantic movies, I actually enjoy action, crime, thriller, and sci-fi movies. Also, I am very interested in cinematography and video editing. I love singing my heart out (in the bathroom) and doing some dancing. I’m up for adventures. I would describe myself as someone who tend to shun the limelight but if needed, count me in. Now, I’m trying to break out of my comfort zone that is why I want to experience new things that I haven’t tried before. In that way, I may discover what more I am capable of.

      I have two brothers and I am the third/youngest child in our family. It was quite tough dealing with two older borthers but at the same time it was fun. Growing up, I really am the type of child who is timid and would prefer to stay at the corner. I was never the type who would talk first when it comes to conversations.


      I started learning to dance with tambourine when I was 7 years old and it was my best friend's mother who taught us. Up until now, I'm still doing the tambourine. Also, now that I kind of mastered the steps already, it's time for me to teach it to the future tambourine dancers of our church's Praise and Worship Team.


      I studied at Philippine Christian Univeristy for my primary education. The school was just walking distance from my home, making it very convenient. Not only that, since my father works at the campus, I have a discount on my tuition fee.

      I graduated high school at the same school, Philippine Christian University. The 4 years I spent in this school was indeed a roller coaster ride with lots of ups and downs. I have learned a lot all those years and there are many memories that I will cherish until the end.



      Last May, I just turned 18. Although it was a tough day since I went to school without having any sleep because I am preparing for the training program that will be held the same day with my birthday, it was nice having your classmates and friends greeting you a happy birthday. It was a memorable day for me because upon going home, I was surprised by my friends with a cake, a banner and a gift. Having all these, I am very grateful for the love that people around me are giving. Also, this reminded me that I should give more love to people around me as well. ♡
