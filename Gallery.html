CHURCH

    This is my home church, Pala-Pala United Methodist Church. I almost grew up in this church for I have been here since I was a little. Before, I used to attend Sunday Schools but now that I'm older, I'm already part of the youth group. This group consists of church members who are 13 to 24 years old. It is divided into three, the freshmen, juniors and seniors. I am part of the Juniors and we call ourselves 'Titanic' which was derived from the word titan. Just like the word itself, it means that our God will always be bigger than our problems.
